Never Waste an Avocado Again: 3 Simple Tricks for Perfect Ripeness

It's a universal struggle: you buy an avocado, and it's either rock-hard or a mushy, brown mess. The window for perfect ripeness can feel impossibly short. But fear not! With these three simple tricks, you can take control of your avocados and enjoy them at their peak every time.

Trick 1: The Stem Test

This is the most reliable way to check an avocado without squeezing (and bruising) it. Try to flick off the small brown stem cap at the top. If it comes off easily and you see a vibrant green underneath, the avocado is ripe and ready to eat. If it's hard to remove, it needs more time. If it comes off and you see brown underneath, it's likely overripe.

Trick 2: Speed Up Ripening

Have a rock-hard avocado that you need for tonight's dinner? Place it in a brown paper bag with a banana or an apple. These fruits release ethylene gas, a natural plant hormone that speeds up the ripening process. Your avocado should be noticeably softer in just a day or two.

Trick 3: Slow Down Ripening

On the flip side, if your avocado is perfectly ripe but you're not ready to eat it, you can slow down the process. Simply place the whole, uncut avocado in the refrigerator. The cold temperature will significantly slow the ripening, giving you several extra days to enjoy it. This simple step is the key to buying ripe avocados without the pressure to eat them immediately.
